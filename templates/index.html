POKHARA RHINOS

“Pokhara Rhinos”, also known as orange army, is a professional cricket team founded in 2016. The team is owned by Jagdamba Sports Pvt. Ltd. which is actively participating in various cricket league such as: Ncell Premier League as “Jagdamba Gaints, Everest Premier League 2016 as Jagdamba Rhinos, and EPL 2017 as Pokhara Rhinos. We have come up with the cause of promoting and supporting sports in Nepal. We belief every sport does matter and the people took sports way forward to national frontier should be honored and respected for the things they had done. Therefore, following and inheriting the same belief ahead to every stakeholder we want you to be part of this initiation through sponsorship for cause. Please take a look at the details we have attached along with this letter. We shall be glad to hear from you.

Jagdamba Sports Pvt. Ltd.

Jagdamba Sports Pvt. Ltd. is a separate legal entity operate under the umbrella of Shanker Group. It has brought the rights of team franchisee from EPL team committee under the name entitled “Pokhara Rhinos”. Jagdamba Sports will have all the rights to plan, operate, and direct each and every activity of Pokhara Rhinos and any one associated with the franchisee. It holds to highest standards of integrity and conducts its every activities and promotions in a fair and unbiased manner. It acts as a platform for providing better opportunities and platforms for sports enthusiast and helps in promoting the legends for the overall development of sports in Nepal.

We take pleasure to bring to your kind notice that Jagdamba Sports Pvt. Ltd., portfolio of Shanker Group, has brought the franchise “Pokhara Rhinos”, a team for Everest Premier League. Everest premier League is a professional cricket league in Nepal played in Twenty-20 format. It is played in round-robin leagues form the group. For the first time in Nepal, EPL Pvt. Ltd is organizing an EPL league in total IPL adaptation format in Kathmandu. This specular league shall typically be relevant platform to cricket players and young talents to showcase their talent and bring them a step ahead in achieving their goal, whereas they will get the opportunity to understand and exchange the cricket culture among the young talents, professional players form team Nepal and foreign players. Not any tournament in the history of Nepal has done this before where each team must have three foreign players in their squad.
